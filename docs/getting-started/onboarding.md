# Onboarding

## Requesting access

1. Create an access request ticket in the ITSM tool.
2. Select *Project – Production* or *Project – Non-prod* as system.
3. Specify your role:
   - Operations Analyst
   - Compliance Officer
   - Support Desk
   - Read-only

## Approval workflow

- Manager approval required for all roles.
- Compliance approval required for elevated access.
- Typical SLA: 1–3 business days.

You will receive an email once your account is provisioned.
