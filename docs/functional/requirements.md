# Functional requirements

## Client management

- FR-01: System shall allow creating, updating, and deactivating clients.
- FR-02: System shall enforce unique client IDs.
- FR-03: System shall track KYC review dates.

## Trade management

- FR-10: System shall allow capturing new trades.
- FR-11: System shall maintain full trade lifecycle history.

## Reporting

- FR-20: System shall provide exports in CSV and XLSX.
