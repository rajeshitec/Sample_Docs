# Functional documentation

Describes **what** the system does in business terms.

## Scope

**In scope**

- Client master data and KYC
- Trade capture and lifecycle
- Approval workflows
- Operational and compliance reporting

**Out of scope**

- Market execution
- Risk analytics / pricing
- External client portals

## Personas

- **Operations Analyst** – maintains client, monitors trades
- **Compliance Officer** – reviews high-risk items
- **Support Desk** – access issues, first-line triage
